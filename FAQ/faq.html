Aftercare

For the first 24–48 hours after your appointment: avoid water, steam, saunas, swimming pools and sweaty workouts. After that, avoid oil-based products around the eye area as oil breaks down the adhesive bond. Never sleep face-down, and try not to rub or pick at your lashes.

Clean your lashes daily using a lash-safe, oil-free foam cleanser and a soft cleansing brush. Gently brush through the lashes and rinse. Clean lashes last longer — buildup of makeup, oil and debris weakens the adhesive and can cause irritation. We stock aftercare products at the studio.

We strongly advise against mascara on lash extensions. Mascara — especially waterproof formulas — weakens the adhesive bond and makes lashes very difficult to clean. One of the great benefits of extensions is that you don't need mascara. If extra volume is needed, book a fill or upgrade your set.

Take care of your investment

Aftercare Guide

Good aftercare means longer retention, healthier natural lashes and better-looking sets between appointments.

✓ DO

The Dos

  • Clean lashes daily with oil-free lash cleanser
  • Brush lashes gently each morning with a spoolie
  • Sleep on your back or use a silk pillowcase
  • Keep fills regular — every 2–3 weeks
  • Book professional removal when needed
  • Arrive with clean, makeup-free eyes
✗ DON'T

The Don'ts

  • Wet lashes within 24–48 hrs of appointment
  • Use oil-based makeup removers or cleansers
  • Rub, pick or pull at your extensions
  • Apply mascara on your extensions
  • Sleep face-down
  • Expose lashes to steam or saunas for long periods
