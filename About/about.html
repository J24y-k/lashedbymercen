How It All Began

A Dream Turned Studio.

Lashed By Mercen was born from a simple but powerful belief, every woman deserves to wake up feeling beautiful, confident, and ready to take on the world. What started as a passion for the art of lash extensions quickly grew into one of Pretoria's most sought,after beauty studios.

Mercen began her journey with a love for precision and detail, training under some of the industry's best and mastering techniques that go far beyond the standard. Every application is thoughtful, every curl angle intentional, every lash placement deliberate.

Today, the studio serves hundreds of loyal clients, women who return not just for the lashes, but for the experience: the calm, the care, and the confidence that walks out the door with every appointment.

2022 Studio founded in Pretoria
