Our Studio Policies

We value your time and ours. Please read our policies before booking to ensure a smooth, enjoyable experience.

    Late Arrival Policy

    A late fee applies if you arrive 15 minutes late. Beyond 15 minutes forfeits your deposit and the appointment is cancelled.

    Arrival Reminder
